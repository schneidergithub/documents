## Definition (practical)
A North Star Metric is a single metric that best captures the value customers derive from your product, and should be a leading indicator of success.  
References:
- https://amplitude.com/blog/good-bad-north-star-metric
- https://amplitude.com/blog/product-north-star-metric

## Use with course objectives
Tie opportunity analysis objectives (revenue/share) to a customer-value outcome metric (NSM) plus input metrics, so discovery and delivery stay aligned.
