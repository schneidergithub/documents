## Definitions (common industry usage)
- **TAM**: total revenue opportunity if you captured 100% of the market.
- **SAM**: portion of TAM you can serve (given product scope/channel/geography).
- **SOM**: portion of SAM you can realistically capture (near-term).  
References:
- https://advertising.amazon.com/library/guides/tam-sam-som
- https://www.salesforce.com/blog/tam-sam-som/

## Use with course market sizing
Pair TAM/SAM/SOM with:
- explicit assumptions
- sensitivity analysis (what changes the estimate materially)
- segment definitions (course requirement).
