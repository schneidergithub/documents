## Definition
RICE is a scoring model for prioritization based on:
Reach, Impact, Confidence, Effort.  
Reference: https://www.intercom.com/blog/rice-simple-prioritization-for-product-managers/

## Use with course
After opportunity analysis produces multiple candidate opportunities/requirements, RICE can help rank initiatives in a consistent way.
