## Quadrants
- Question Mark (low share, high growth)
- Star (high share, high growth)
- Cash Cow (high share, low growth)
- Out to Pasture (low share, low growth)  
Source: Session 2. (S2 p35)

## Example guidance (from slide)
- Question Mark: product/process development; customer segmentation/communication.
- Star: identify customers/requirements; innovate to meet requirements and differentiate.
- Cash Cow: keep costs low; control business relationships; hold share/margins.
- Out to Pasture: improve profit via cost cuts and/or gaining customers; plan for retirement.
