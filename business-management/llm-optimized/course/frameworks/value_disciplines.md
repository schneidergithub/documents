## Model
Three ways to create value and differentiation:
- Operational excellence
- Customer intimacy
- Product leadership  
Source: Session 2. (S2 p33)

## Usage
Pick a primary discipline as your dominant differentiation strategy; use it to constrain roadmap, pricing, service model, and messaging.
