## Stack (top-down)
Vision  
→ Corporate Strategy  
→ Divisional Strategy  
→ Product/Market Strategy  
→ Product Objectives  
→ Marketing Tactics  
→ Customer satisfaction  
Source: Session 2. (S2 p40)

## Use
When a product decision is debated, identify which layer it belongs to and ensure it does not contradict higher layers.
