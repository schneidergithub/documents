## Roles
- **Timekeeper**: knows the allocated time and keeps the team aware of time passage. (S2 p13)
- **Scribe**: records the team’s results. (S2 p13)
- **Facilitator**: understands the activity goal, keeps focus, ensures participation. (S2 p13)

## CS mapping (optional)
- timekeeper → scheduler / timebox enforcer
- scribe → logger / persistent store
- facilitator → orchestrator / control-loop moderator
