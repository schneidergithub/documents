## Process (what you produce)

### Inputs
- Objectives: revenue/profitability; market share. (S3 p11)
- Strategy lenses (examples): Ansoff, TALC, BCG, Tong & Zagula, Kotler. (S3 p11)

### Activities (core artifacts)
- Problems to be solved
- Target segments
- Target competitors
- Value proposition
- Positioning statement
- High-level requirements
- Opportunity size estimate  
Source: Session 3 (Opportunity Analysis Process). (S3 p11)

### Outputs (beyond the activity list)
Strategic + operational elements include:
- Markets/segments/targets
- Competitive analysis & targets
- Whole-product components
- Value proposition + positioning
- Operations/service/support
- Business model
- Sales & marketing strategy
- Business case (NPV, pricing, units/forecast, costs)
- Risk analysis
- Budget required for next stage  
Source: Session 3 (Framework for Opportunity Analysis). (S3 p12)

## Minimal checklist (agent-friendly)
- [ ] Objectives stated (revenue/profitability and/or share)
- [ ] Strategy lens selected and justified
- [ ] Problem statement(s) drafted (buyer/user/influencer as needed)
- [ ] Competitive set identified
- [ ] Segment definition + sizing approach documented
- [ ] Value prop + positioning captured
- [ ] High-level requirements captured
- [ ] Business case + risk + next-stage budget captured
